Medium Strategy

Comparing Media Options

I thought through a few different ways to share my message about wrestling, resilience, and hard work. I tried to picture what each option would really feel like for me to make.

Website

A website felt like something I could actually handle. I can split my ideas into pages, and people can read it when they want. I already know basic HTML and CSS, so it doesn't feel overwhelming.

Blog Article

I also thought about writing a blog article. It would be simple and easy to post, but it would all be on one long page. That makes it harder to separate different parts of my message.

Video Essay

Another option was a video essay. It could be interesting, but I don't have editing skills or good equipment. I also know that people have to sit and watch the whole thing, which doesn't always happen.

Podcast

I considered a podcast too. It sounds cool, but I don't have recording tools, and I would need to edit audio. Adults might not have time to listen all the way through, so it doesn't feel like the best fit.

My Choice: Website

In the end, a website makes the most sense for me. I can build it with the skills I already have, and it lets me organize my thoughts clearly. It also fits how I want people to read my story and think about wrestling, resilience, and hard work.
