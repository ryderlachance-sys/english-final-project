Medium Strategy

Comparing Media Options

I considered several different media for sharing my message about wrestling:

Website

A website allows me to organize my story across multiple pages. Adults can read at their own pace and share it easily. I can create simple HTML and CSS pages, which fits my abilities.

Blog Article

A blog article would allow for detailed, thoughtful reading. However, it would be harder to organize multiple sections and examples compared to a website with separate pages.

Video Essay

A video essay could be engaging, but it requires watching time and audio/video access. I don't have video editing skills or equipment, so this wouldn't work for me.

Podcast

A podcast could reach people who like to listen, but it requires dedicated listening time that busy adults may not have. I also don't have recording equipment or editing software.

My Choice: Website

I chose a website because it works well for my audience and fits my abilities. Adults can access it easily, read at their own pace, and share it with others. I can create simple HTML and CSS pages to organize my message clearly across multiple pages. A website appears in both the list of media that works for my audience and the list of media I can actually create, making it the best choice.
