Exemplar Analysis

I looked at two real examples of websites that share personal messages like mine. Below I explain what works well and what doesn't work well in each example.

Example 1: USA Wrestling - "Life Lessons Learned Through Wrestling"

Author/Organization: USA Wrestling

Website: TheMat.com

URL: https://www.themat.com/news/2019/july/18/life-lessons-learned-through-wrestling

What Works Well:

  • The article stays focused on clear life lessons, which makes the message easy to understand
  • It uses sports examples that connect to real life, which fits my topic
  • The tone is straightforward and appropriate for a general audience
  • The structure is simple and easy to follow

What Does Not Work Well:

  • Some points are short and could use more detail or examples
  • The article feels more informational than personal
  • It could use clearer transitions between ideas

Example 2: Shore Local News - "Life Lessons From the Wrestling Mat"

Author/Organization: Shore Local News

Website: ShoreLocalNews.com

URL: https://shorelocalnews.com/life-lessons-from-the-wrestling-mat/

What Works Well:

  • The topic connects wrestling to life lessons, which matches my message
  • The writing is clear and easy to follow
  • Examples about sportsmanship and resilience are easy to understand
  • The article stays focused on one main idea

What Does Not Work Well:

  • Some ideas are repeated in similar ways
  • It could include more personal details to make it feel more relatable
  • The ending feels short and could be stronger
