Possible Messages

Based on my personal experiences with wrestling, here are possible messages I could communicate:

Wrestling helps clear my mind

When I'm on the mat, I have to focus completely on the match. This helps me forget about stress and worries, and my mind feels clearer after practice.

Wrestling teaches me how to handle losses

I lose matches sometimes, and wrestling has taught me to learn from losses instead of just feeling bad about them. This helps me in other parts of my life too.

Wrestling helps me stay mentally healthy

Wrestling gives me a way to deal with stress and build resilience. It's not just a sport, it's a way to stay mentally healthy.
